Bright Steps International School (BSI), is a recently established inclusive school for children with Autism and their neurotypical counterparts.

Learning opportunities for every child

We provide neurotypical and atypical teaching experience, with a stimulating academic curriculum, matched to their abilities, in a clean, serene, caring, learning environment combined with a unique international focus.
